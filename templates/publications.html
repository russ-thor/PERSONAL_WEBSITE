How Burns Lake Community Forest Leveraged LiDAR Analysis and Visual Magnitude to Support Decision Making

This article, published as the cover story in the Spring 2021 British Columbia Forest Professional (BCFP) Magazine, describes how Light Detection and Ranging (LiDAR) data can be used to improve Visual Resource Management (VRM) in BC. The benefits described include:

  • Significant accuracy improvements in elevation and tree height model inputs
  • Ability to perform detailed visibility analysis on the landbase
  • Improved statistics and data analytics reporting
  • Greater confidence in professional management decisions
